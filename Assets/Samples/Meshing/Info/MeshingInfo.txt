This scene generates a mesh of your surroundings.

<b>Instructions</b>:

Just move your camera around, a mesh should generate around all the objects and planes detected.

To activate Meshing filtering, select one of the options in the Settings button.
"Allow Ground only" will only generate meshes off that match the semantic channel for ground.
"Block recommended" will block the semantic channels of: sky, person water and pets. And generate meshes for everything else.
